Inventory Planning (what we offer)

Software that helps forecast inventory needs based on historical sales data and trends

✅ How It Helps

  • Identifying stock issues before they become issues
  • Reducing time spent digging through data manually
  • Taking confident markdowns

❌ When It Doesn't Help

  • For new retailers without adequate data history
  • Stores too small (below $200k annual revenue)

Min-Max

Simple system setting minimum and maximum inventory levels

✅ How It Helps

  • Managing replenishment for predictable, low-complexity inventory needs
  • Quick, simple methods to prevent stockouts

❌ When It Doesn't Help

  • For high-variety, high-complexity inventory needs
  • Cannot adjust for trends or unique demand patterns

POS + Excel

Uses basic POS data combined with Excel spreadsheets to manage inventory

✅ How It Helps

  • Provides visibility to inventory without needing specialized software
  • Simple and inexpensive solution for managing inventory basics

❌ When It Doesn't Help

  • Lack of automation makes it error-prone
  • Difficult to scale for anything over $200k

Assortment Planning

Optimizes product mix based on demand, trends, and sales goals

✅ How It Helps

  • When deciding what products to carry in a store
  • Determining the best assortment to maximize sales and meet customer demand

❌ When It Doesn't Help

  • Lacks day-to-day inventory visibility
  • Not ideal for managing detailed stock levels or reordering needs
